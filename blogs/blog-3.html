Computer Vision enables machines to interpret and analyze visual information. From face recognition to medical imaging, CV systems are everywhere.

Core Techniques

  • Preprocessing and enhancement
  • Feature extraction (SIFT, HOG)
  • Detection (Haar cascades, YOLO)
  • Segmentation (U-Net, Mask R-CNN)

Applications

  • Healthcare diagnostics and imaging
  • Autonomous driving and robotics
  • Quality inspection in manufacturing
  • AR/VR and retail analytics

As hardware improves and datasets grow, computer vision continues to advance at a rapid pace.
