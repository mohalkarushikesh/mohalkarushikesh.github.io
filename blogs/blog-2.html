Deep Learning represents the cutting edge of AI, using neural networks with multiple layers to process complex patterns in data. This approach has transformed computer vision, NLP, speech, and more.

Neural Network Basics

  • Activation functions (ReLU, sigmoid, tanh)
  • Loss functions and optimization (SGD, Adam)
  • Regularization (dropout, weight decay, batch norm)

Architectures

  • CNNs for images (convolutions, pooling, residual blocks)
  • RNNs/LSTMs/GRUs for sequences
  • Transformers for attention-based modeling

Training Considerations

  • Data preprocessing and augmentation
  • Hyperparameter tuning and learning rate schedules
  • Monitoring metrics and early stopping

With modern frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow, building and training deep models is more accessible than ever.
